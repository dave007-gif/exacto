About Exacto

Our Mission

To empower builders, planners, and contractors with the tools they need to get precise estimates of building materials — both in quantity and cost. We believe accurate data should be easy, fast, and accessible.

What We Do

Exacto simplifies the estimating process through smart automation. Our platform helps you:

  • ✅ Analyze your building plans quickly
  • 📊 Get detailed breakdowns of material quantities
  • 💰 Know the exact costs before you build
  • 📁 Export or share estimates with clients or team members

Who We Help

Whether you're a solo builder, a construction firm, or a project planner, Exacto fits right into your workflow — cutting down manual effort and boosting your accuracy.

Our Vision

We see a future where every construction decision is backed by smart data — no guesswork, no overbuying, no waste. Just the right materials, at the right cost, every time.
